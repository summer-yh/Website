Travel Diary

Bali, Indonesia

A few favorite places from my trip to Bali, captured through photos and short diary notes.

Bali, Indonesia

Unagi Mas Villas

Unagi Mas Villas felt like a quiet retreat. The villa was beautiful, with a lovely pool and cozy bedrooms that made it feel peaceful and private at the same time.

It was the kind of place that immediately made the trip feel slower, softer, and more relaxing.

Bali, Indonesia

Ubud Monkey Forest

The Monkey Forest felt full of life. It was lush, green, and shaded by tropical trees, with monkeys everywhere and winding paths that made the whole place feel immersive.

It was one of those places where every corner seemed to have a little movement, sound, or surprise.

Bali, Indonesia

Ubud Palace

Ubud Palace stood out because of its exquisite historical monuments and the richness of its colors. The carved details and ornate decorations made the whole place feel vibrant and full of history.

It felt like a place where architecture and atmosphere worked together beautifully.
